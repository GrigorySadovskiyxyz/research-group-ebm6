Master's Programme in Industrial Engineering and Management

Industrial engineering and management combines business, technology and leadership studies. This is how to train corporate developers who are not afraid to tackle challenging problems.

In LUT, you can study Industrial Engineering and Management in the Master's programme of Global Management of Innovation and Technology.

As an expert in both technology and business, you will know how business enterprises work and how to make them work even better. The programme will provide you with tools to work in an international, constantly changing and networked environment.
